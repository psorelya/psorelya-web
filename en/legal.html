03 — Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

In effect since January 1, 2025

Your satisfaction is our priority. If for any reason you are not fully satisfied with your order, here is how we proceed.

Return window

You have 30 days from receipt of your order to contact us to initiate a return. After this period, we will unfortunately be unable to process your request.

Return conditions

  • Products must be returned in their original packaging, unopened and unused
  • Products damaged upon receipt are eligible for a full refund — a photo is required within 48 hours of delivery
  • Products purchased on sale are not refundable, except for manufacturing defects
Exception — skin reaction: We understand that every skin is unique. If you experience an unusual skin reaction after using a Psorelya product, contact us immediately at soin@psorelya.com — we will review your situation on a case-by-case basis with all the attention it deserves.

Return procedure

To initiate a return, send an email to soin@psorelya.com with your order number and reason for return. We will respond within 2 business days with shipping instructions.

Refund

Once the return is received and validated, the refund will be processed via the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days. Original shipping costs are not refunded unless the error is ours.

Health Disclaimer

The products and information presented on this website are intended to complement, not replace, professional medical care.

Nature of Psorelya products

Psorelya products are natural cosmetic care formulated for the daily comfort of sensitive and atopic skin. They are not medications and are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.

Editorial content and articles

Articles published in the Learn section of our website are written for informational and educational purposes only. They reflect the founder's personal research and experience, and do not constitute medical advice in any way.

Always consult a healthcare professional — doctor, dermatologist or pharmacist — before making changes to your skincare routine, especially in cases of diagnosed skin conditions, pregnancy, breastfeeding, or medication use.

Allergies and skin reactions

We recommend performing a patch test on a small area before first use. In case of redness, irritation or discomfort, discontinue use and consult a professional.

Individual results

Testimonials reflect individual experiences and do not guarantee similar results for all users. Skin response to cosmetic care varies according to genetics, lifestyle and many other factors.
